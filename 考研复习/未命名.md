设 $X$ 是连续型随机变量, 设 $U=F_{X}(X)$. 则 $F^{-1}_{X}(x)$ 是 $F_{X}(x)$ 的反函数.
$$
\begin{align}
&F_{U}(u) = P\left\{ U \leq u \right\} = P\left\{F_{X}(X)\leq u  \right\} =P\left\{ X \leq F^{-1}_{X}(u) \right\}  =F_{X}(F^{-1}_{X}(u)) = u
\end{align}
$$
故 $U\sim U(0,1)$
